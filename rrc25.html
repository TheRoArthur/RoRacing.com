The Roblox Racing Convention 2025, taking place in January, will bring together racing fans, developers, and RoRacers for an exciting celebration of virtual racing.
The convention will feature booths from top leagues, game developers, and racing enthusiasts, offering exclusive previews of new content and insights into the world of Roblox racing.
Whether you're looking to discover the latest in racing game technology or network with other racing fans, this convention will be the ultimate hub for the Roblox racing scene.
